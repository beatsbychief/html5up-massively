Quick Bio

Ryan Moccasin started producing music in 2007 and has since
branched out to release several Hip Hop albums in Canada. As an Indigenous
music producer, he is needed more than ever to remind us of the dark chapters
of our collective history. He has spent more than ten years studying music and
the positive outcomes it can produce for a fragmented society.
Over that period, he has produced songs for Saskatoon artists such as
“Crash Course - Cquel” (March 2016) and “Music in My Room - Kurlz” (March 2016),
and has contributed to their respective albums as a beat maker.

As a constant student of music, he has also learned how to perform live as a DJ.
This approach has taken him on tour throughout western Canada.
This has also led him to branch out as a music producer where he has explored different genres
such as Rock and Electronic. He is currently working on two new tracks to release called “OK Move” and “Count On Me”.

In addition, he also offers his services on music job site Soundbetter.com. Contact above for more information.
